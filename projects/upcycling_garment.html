This project is an up-cycled garment made from three garment found from a thrift store.

I had the idea of combining the jacket and the shirt to a shirt dress at the beginning, but the color choice of red really reminded me of the Chinese New Year just past, also red and black is a classic color combination in many designs of Chinese style, so I decided to add some Chinese elements into it.

I wanted this garment to be something wearable in everyday life, so I didn't have any exaggerated design, and it works well both as a jacket and a dress.

I tried to express some feelings of the 1920s of China in this group of photos, but it is also a combination of vintage and a new tide of fashion. So I used many props and filters that can bring people back to the time, but the accessories I chose are actually new tides.

Finally, I need to thank my friend Weijia Yin for helping me as the model.
