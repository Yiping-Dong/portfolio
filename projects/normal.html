In this film, I am discussing the idea of "what is normal".

People always judge others based on their own standards, and a lot of them don't accept any challenge.

This piece is originally from my own experience of "hating the sun while living close to the beach" and developed more when I saw the news of the Chinese government banning the LGBTQ social media accounts.


I wrote the story and the script all by myself, also directed, shot most of it, and was on camera myself.

It wasn't easy to design all the shots, take care of the camera, and act all by myself, but that is what I have to challenge myself on in the scenario of taking classes online during the pandemic.

It is a special experience of producing a short film almost without any help from others and only using cell phones as cameras.

It wasn't easy, but I learned a lot during the progress.


A huge thanks to my friend Milin for helping me as a cinematographer on some shots!
