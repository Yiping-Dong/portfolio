Kitchen helper is a 16 pages miniature book, only black and white. Dimension of the physical book is 4.25in by 2.75in.

This book include a section on history of dumplings, my memoir relating to dumplings, and a recipe of dumplings.

The title Kitchen Helpers is the title of the memoir. I named it Kitchen Helpers because making dumplings needs many helpers and I am usually one of the helpers when my family make dumplings.
