This is an animation with the theme of Wuxia(Chinese Martial Art) made with Maya

I modeled the architectures first then designed the characters.

I put the characters into Mixamo and generated the joints, and used a plug-in to generate the rigs.

There were originally sleeves and swords designed. However, nCloth sleeves doesn't work well and it will require remodeling everything.
